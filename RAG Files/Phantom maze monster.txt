Phantom maze monster was the 208th event of Project SEKAI COLORFUL STAGE!. This event focuses on the character from Wonderlands×Showtime. The event period started on June 20th 2026 and ended on June 28th, 2026.

Summary
One day after a meeting, the members of Wonderlands×Showtime decided to stage their very own show. With Sakaki's help, they were able to successfully secure a theater and performed their show smoothly on the first day. However, after the show, Sakaki came up to them and said: "This show can still be improved. Do you mind if I lend you a hand?"...
