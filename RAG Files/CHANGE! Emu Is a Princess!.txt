CHANGE! Emu Is a Princess!? (CHANGE！えむは王女さま！？, CHANGE! Emu wa Ojou-sama!?) was the 196th event of Project SEKAI COLORFUL STAGE!. The focus of this event was on the characters from Wonderlands×Showtime. The event period started on February 19th, 2026, and ended on February 26th, 2026.

Summary
After their successful performance, the members of Wonderlands×Showtime decided to take a break by going to ArcLand for fun. During their visit, Emu is excited to learn more about amusement park management, but she unexpectedly runs into a girl who looks exactly like her instead…
