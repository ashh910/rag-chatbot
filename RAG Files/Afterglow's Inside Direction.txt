Afterglow's Inside Direction (残照のInside Direction, Zanshou no Inside Direction) was the 200th event of Project SEKAI COLORFUL STAGE!. This event focuses on the characters from Wonderlands×Showtime. The event period started on March 30th, 2026 and ended on April 7th, 2026.

Summary
After completing their training and having their energy fully restored, the members of Wonderlands×Showtime are now considering which path they should take next. Just then, they receive a call from Director Ohara—whom they had worked with on a film project a while back—and to their surprise, the director wants Rui to appear in a commercial for a TV drama as... an actor...!?
