Deep Dark For Light was the 178th event of Project SEKAI COLORFUL STAGE!. The focus of this event was on the characters from Wonderlands×Showtime. The event period started on August 30th and ended on September 4th, 2025.

Summary
On the last day of the workshop, the members of Wonderlands×Showtime learned that a contest called "ArcLand Show Contest" will be held soon. Seeing this as an opportunity, Tsukasa decided to participate in the contest. Faced with the challenging role of a despairing prince, Tsukasa soon realized the huge gap between him and Asahi...
